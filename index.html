Hi there! My name is Jazzie Flom, and both music and people are two of my greatest passions in life! I have been playing guitar and singing for fifteen years, and picked up the ukulele about seven years ago. After teaching music lessons for roughly four years now, I am sure it is something I want to do for the rest of my life. In order to further develop my craft and teaching skills, I am currently studying jazz guitar at Long Beach City College with Michael Higgins. I am working toward transferring to Cal State Long Beach, where I hope to finish with my performance degree in jazz studies.

If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact me! I am looking forward to meeting and jamming with you ;)

-Jazzie
